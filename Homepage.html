About the questionaire

Our questionaire consists of questions which comes under fantasy if we are talking with respect to genre.Overall our questionaire basically aims to give a fun experience to all the users out there.The end point of our questionaire is a character which the user has attained depending on their choices which they have chosen during the questionaire process.
